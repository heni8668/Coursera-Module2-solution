Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (born 5 February 1985), better known as Cristiano Ronaldo, or by his nickname ' CR7 ', is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward. He plays for Premier League club Manchester United and is the captain of the Portuguese national team.
